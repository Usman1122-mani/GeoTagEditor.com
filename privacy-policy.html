1. Introduction

Welcome to GeotagEditor.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and have designed our service from the ground up to respect your privacy rights.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and services. The short version: we don't collect your personal data because our tool works entirely in your browser.

2. Information We Collect

We Collect Nothing

Unlike most websites and online services, GeotagEditor.com is designed to operate without collecting any personal information from you. Here's what this means:

2.1 Your Images

Your photos are processed 100% in your web browser using JavaScript. They are never:

  • Uploaded to our servers
  • Transmitted over the internet
  • Stored on our systems
  • Accessed by us or any third parties

The processing happens locally on your device, similar to how a desktop application works.

2.2 Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information including:

  • • Name
  • • Email address
  • • Phone number
  • • Physical address
  • • IP address
  • • Browser information
  • • Usage statistics
  • • Location data

2.3 Cookies and Tracking

We do not use:

  • Cookies for tracking purposes
  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media trackers
  • Session storage for tracking

3. How Our Service Works

100% Client-Side Processing

Here's the technical explanation of how Geotag Editor protects your privacy:

3.1 Image Processing

  1. You select an image file from your device
  2. The image is loaded into your browser's memory using JavaScript File API
  3. GPS coordinates are added or removed using EXIF metadata libraries
  4. The modified image is created entirely in your browser
  5. You download the processed image to your device

At no point in this process does the image data leave your browser or get transmitted over the internet.

3.2 Server Interaction

Our servers only provide:

  • The initial HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files to load the website
  • Static assets (icons, fonts, etc.)
  • No processing of your images happens on our servers

4. Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services to keep our website running:

Cloudflare

We use Cloudflare as a CDN (Content Delivery Network) to serve our static files faster. Cloudflare may see your IP address when you request our website files, but they do not receive any information about your image processing activities.

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to display typography. When you load our website, your browser may request font files from Google's servers.

Map Services (Optional)

If you choose to use the interactive map feature, map tiles are loaded from third-party map providers. This is optional and only occurs if you interact with the map.

5. Data Security

The best security is not collecting data in the first place. Since we don't collect or store your data:

No Data Breach Risk

We can't lose or leak data we don't collect. There's no database of user information to compromise.

End-to-End Security

Your images remain on your device. The security of your data depends on your device's security, not ours.

5.1 Your Responsibility

Since all processing happens on your device:

  • Ensure your device has up-to-date security software
  • Use a secure internet connection when loading our website
  • Keep your browser updated to the latest version
  • Clear browser cache if using a public computer

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is suitable for users of all ages because we don't collect personal information. However, we recommend that children under 13 use our service with parental guidance.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Important: We will never change our policy to start collecting your personal data without your explicit consent and prominent notification.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, you can contact us at:

Email: privacy@geotageditor.com

Please note: We don't have a physical office address because we're a fully remote team building privacy-focused web tools.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Even though we don't collect your data, you still have rights regarding your privacy:

Right to Access

You can verify we have no data about you by examining our open-source code or contacting us.

Right to Deletion

Since we don't store your data, there's nothing to delete. Your data remains under your control.

Right to Object

You can stop using our service at any time. Since we don't track you, stopping usage is immediate.

Verify Our Privacy Claims

We believe in transparency. Here's how you can verify that we're telling the truth about our privacy practices:

Review Our Code

Our entire codebase is open source on GitHub. You can review exactly how the tool works.

Test Network Traffic

Use browser developer tools to verify no image data is sent to our servers.

We welcome scrutiny because we have nothing to hide. Privacy is our product.
